Why are themes important? To allow the Users pick visuals that They like, as opposed to (or in conjunction with) what the graphic designer liked. In the long run, to keep User interest and avoid boredom (e.g. via random theme), therefore increasing the utility value of the product. Thus, same product, but more retention, better product-user fit, better ROI. Flat? 3D? Material? Neumorphism? Neubrutalism? Light? Dark? The design trends change cyclically. This demo is flexible enough to accommodate the changes. Without even code modifications. Without even reloading the page. And allowing the End-Users to tweak it to their preferences.
